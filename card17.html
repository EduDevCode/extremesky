Question: A car takes a turn, the driver feels that he is being pulled to the opposite side of the turn. Explain the fact in one word:


Answer: Inertia.



You answered correctly: Move forward 1 position!


You answered incorrectly: Go back 1 position! If you are in position 1, nothing happens.
