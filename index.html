Game Rules

  • Only get a card if the number on the dice is less than or equal to 4.

  • If the dice number is greater than or equal to 5, you must advance 3 positions.

  • This is a game for up to 4 players.

  • If you take a card, you cannot take any more until the end of the round.

  • When a card is removed from the pile, it can only be placed again when there are no cards available.

  • Prohibited for children under 3 years of age as it contains small parts that can be swallowed.

  • The numbers at the top of the cards do not refer to the numbering of the positions. They are just to facilitate the answers conference and presentation of rewards or punishments.

  • The cards must be shuffled and drawn at random.
